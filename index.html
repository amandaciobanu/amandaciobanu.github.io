ABOUT ME

At the beginning of 2020, I decided to pursue a new and exciting career in software development. To fulfill this objective, I attended Le Wagon Bootcamp (full-time) in Lisbon which I have graduated in July 2020 (batch #419).

During the 9 weeks of the Bootcamp, I have found myself passionate about the entire process of creating a web application, including prototyping, deploying, and the inevitable bug-fixing.

The learning process never stops - I am continuously working on deepening the skills I acquired during the Bootcamp and acquiring new ones that were not part of the curriculum, such as front-end frameworks like React and Svelte.

Looking forward to my next challenge where I can become a great developer, work on meaningful products, and be an active member of the tech community.
